Language: Dutch
Abstract:

Edward Upward mentions that he is sending his copy of the Crown edition of Journey to The Border to Léon Perrin of Avignon who thinks of translating the book abnd wishes to know about the copyright. Upward asks whether he can have the permission of the Hogarth Press to give the go ahead to give the necessary information before Perrin's son is returning to France on the 20th of December.



Dulwich S.E.21 , 12th Dec '45


Dear Miss Hepworth,


Thank you for sending back
The Postal Order. I am sorry I misunderstood the Account you
sent me.

I am sending my copy of the Crown edition
of Journey to The Border to Léon Perrin of Avignon
who is thinking of translating it for 'Confluences'.
He wants to know details about copyright. I should
be glad if you could let me have these and also if
you would let me know whether I have the permission
of The Hogarth Press to tell him to go ahead with the
translation if he wishes to do so. His son whom I have
met in London is returning to France next Thursday
Dec 20th and I should like to have the necessary
information before then.


Yours sincerely,

Edward Upward

Edward Upward 154 Turney Rd
